Do you want to know the correct way to name versions? That's what `PEP 440`_ is for: Version Identification and Dependency Specification.

Perhaps the most noteworthy thing is the use of those suffixes .dev0, etc. for eggs in development.


.. _PEP 440: https://www.python.org/dev/peps/pep-0440/
