Middle click in firefox

Posted on Fri 01 January 2016 in Systems

Another reminder note. I usually open links in a new window in firefox using the middle button. It often happens to me that I have a url in the clipboard and firefox then opens it for me instead of the link. To avoid this, simply go to about:config and set the property middlemouse.contentLoadURL to false.
