os.click: Operating System ISO Download Center

Posted on Thu, 12 Mar 2026 in Programación

Next time I need a Windows ISO or some Linux distro and don't feel like spending half an hour jumping between Microsoft's menus or searching for the right mirror, I should go straight to os.click.

I needed a Windows 11 ISO for a VM today, and as usual, I couldn't remember the exact URL. This site saved the day. What's good about it:

  • Direct links: They don't host anything; they just point to the official servers (Microsoft, Canonical, etc.).
  • Clean: No weird ads or fake "Download" buttons.
  • Variety: They have Windows (10, 11, Server) and almost any Linux distro I normally use.

Basically, it's a well-organized index. Just for my future self.
